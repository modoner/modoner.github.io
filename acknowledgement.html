This website has been prepared to provide information about the activities undertaken by Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region and also to an extent about the North Eastern States in general and infrastructure creation in them in particular. 

 

The information under the silos ‘Infrastructure’ & ‘About North East’ has been prepared on the basis of information, presentations, websites of the concerned Ministries/Departments/ agencies of the Central Government and State Governments. Our Mandate
Our vision is to foster a culturally rich people by creating meaningful interaction with the arts, generating appreciation for cultural heritage and restoring dignity in crafts practice. Happy Hands is committed towards the revival of traditional/folk art and craft, empowering rural artisans and building potential in women and young people to lead community development activities and craft entrepreneurship.
