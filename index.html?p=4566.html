Status & Future Plans

Airports In The North-East Region

The North Eastern Region has an extensive network of airports and air fields which are a legacy of the IInd World War. After Independence, the Ministry of Civil Aviation and North Eastern Council have cooperated to develop a good infrastructure of airports. There are uncovered areas that need infrastructure for air connectivity.
